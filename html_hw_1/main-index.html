Why I'm here..

I am attending Coding Temple so I can begin a career in software engineering. I was never a tech-savy person, nor did I have much intrest in technology. Infact, I touted to my friends how little time I spent on computers, video games, or other devices. My brother, who is a software engineer, persistantly told me to consider software engineering since he knew I have been struggling to find my ideal career after I graduated college. Finally, my brother wore me down and convinced me to take an online course from a website called Udemy. The course was about HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and I became hooked imediatly. After several months of study, I reduced my hours at the brewery to part time and am now attending Coding Temple. I am eager to progress in the trade and thrilled to see where it can take me.
